Result Types¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2474#1{{}}\mathdef2474{result type} & \href{../text/types.html#text-resulttype}{\mathtt{resulttype}} &::=& (t{:}\href{../text/types.html#text-functype}{\mathtt{result}})^? &\Rightarrow& [t^?] \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Note

In future versions of WebAssembly, this scheme may be extended to support multiple results or more general result types.

Abbreviations¶

Multiple anonymous parameters or results may be combined into a single declaration:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef2474#1{{}}\mathdef2474{parameter} & \def\mathdef2526#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2526{(}~~\def\mathdef2527#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2527{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef2528#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2528{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef2529#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2529{(}~~\def\mathdef2530#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2530{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef2531#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2531{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef2474#1{{}}\mathdef2474{result} & \def\mathdef2532#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2532{(}~~\def\mathdef2533#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2533{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef2534#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2534{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef2535#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2535{(}~~\def\mathdef2536#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2536{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef2537#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2537{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
