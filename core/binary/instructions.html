Instructions¶

Instructions are encoded by opcodes. Each opcode is represented by a single byte, and is followed by the instruction’s immediate arguments, where present. The only exception are structured control instructions, which consist of several opcodes bracketing their nested instruction sequences.

Note

Gaps in the byte code ranges for encoding instructions are reserved for future extensions.

Control Instructions¶

Control instructions have varying encodings. For structured instructions, the instruction sequences forming nested blocks are terminated with explicit opcodes for \(\href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{end}}\) and \(\href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{else}}\).

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef635#1{{}}\mathdef635{instruction} & \href{../binary/instructions.html#binary-instr}{\mathtt{instr}} &::=& \def\mathdef671#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef671{00} &\Rightarrow& \href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{unreachable}} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef672#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef672{01} &\Rightarrow& \href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{nop}} \\ &&|& &\Rightarrow& \href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{block}}~\mathit{rt}~\mathit{in}^\ast~\href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{end}} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef675#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef675{03}~~\mathit{rt}{:}\href{../binary/types.html#binary-blocktype}{\mathtt{blocktype}}~~(\mathit{in}{:}\href{../binary/instructions.html#binary-instr}{\mathtt{instr}})^\ast~~\def\mathdef676#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef676{0B} &\Rightarrow& \href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{loop}}~\mathit{rt}~\mathit{in}^\ast~\href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{end}} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef677#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef677{04}~~\mathit{rt}{:}\href{../binary/types.html#binary-blocktype}{\mathtt{blocktype}}~~(\mathit{in}{:}\href{../binary/instructions.html#binary-instr}{\mathtt{instr}})^\ast~~\def\mathdef678#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef678{0B} &\Rightarrow& \href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{if}}~\mathit{rt}~\mathit{in}^\ast~\href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{else}}~\epsilon~\href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{end}} \\ &&|& Reference Instructions¶

Reference instructions are represented by single byte codes.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef635#1{{}}\mathdef635{instruction} & \href{../binary/instructions.html#binary-instr}{\mathtt{instr}} &::=& \dots \\ &&|& \def\mathdef689#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef689{D0} &\Rightarrow& \href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-ref}{\mathsf{ref{.}null}} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef690#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef690{D1} &\Rightarrow& \href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-ref}{\mathsf{ref{.}is\_null}} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef691#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef691{D2}~~x{:}\href{../binary/modules.html#binary-funcidx}{\mathtt{funcidx}} &\Rightarrow& \href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-ref}{\mathsf{ref{.}func}}~x \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Note

These opcode assignments are preliminary.

Parametric Instructions¶

Parametric instructions are represented by single byte codes.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef635#1{{}}\mathdef635{instruction} & \href{../binary/instructions.html#binary-instr}{\mathtt{instr}} &::=& \dots \\ &&|& \def\mathdef692#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef692{1A} &\Rightarrow& \href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-parametric}{\mathsf{drop}} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef693#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef693{1B} &\Rightarrow& \href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-parametric}{\mathsf{select}} \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Variable instructions are represented by byte codes followed by the encoding of the respective index.

Expressions¶

Expressions are encoded by their instruction sequence terminated with an explicit \(\def\mathdef861#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef861{0B}\) opcode for \(\href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-control}{\mathsf{end}}\).
